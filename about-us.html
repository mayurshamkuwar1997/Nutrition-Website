Production Process ▼

The production process begins with the selection of raw materials having high nutritional value. Incoming raw-material passes through our labs for testing. Then the raw-material & premixes of medicines is mixed in the mixer under the strict supervision as per the formulation of the product. After this, analyst check the products in the lab, certain test are conducted for their analysis. After this, the material is packed in bags & stored in the dry place. We strictly follow our standards and abide by the quality and quantity process.

High Quality Raw Material Standards ▼

At Shresta Nutritions we realize what goes in comes out as well. Hence, Shresta ensures that all its products are produced from the finest ingredients. Shresta production systems are set up to adhere to the principle of total quality.

Our Feed is produced to the highest technical and quality standards in state-of-the art facilities. Each batch is analyzed for the content of ingredient and bio-availability with latest technology.

About Shresta Nutritions

Shresta Nutritions is a Bangalore based poulrty feed supplying company, we are strongly committed to offer high quality poultry feed to the poultry farmers and support them to bring down their feeding costs by providing them quality products at the best price in the market.

We supply Poultry feed for farm poultry, including, chickens, ducks, geese and other domestic birds. The quantity and nutritional requirement of the feed depend on the weight and the age of the poultry as well as the season. Healthy poultry requires a sufficient amount of protein and carbohydrates, along with the necessary vitamins, dietary minerals, and an adequate supply of water.
